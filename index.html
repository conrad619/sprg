OUR STORY

Since our founding in 1995, we at Strategic Public Relations Group (SPRG) have sought to make a difference. This pursuit has both inspired and driven us, leading to the creation of the largest public relations consultancy in Hong Kong and one of the largest public relations networks in Asia. With over 320 professionals working from 15 offices in Hong Kong, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Taiwan, Singapore and Malaysia, complemented by a cohesive global affiliation network covering over 140 cities, we provide clients, both local and international, with a comprehensive range of world-class public relations services.

Indicative of our leadership of the IPO communications segment in Hong Kong, we have completed 430 campaigns to date. And as shrewd as we are at corporate communications, we are just as adept at advising clients on public, investor, media and government relations; offering a full gamut of services, including corporate and marketing communications, issues and crisis management, event management and digital marketing services. We also provide media and presentation skills training, design and content creation, and editorial support; rounding off our full breadth of client-centric solutions.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Committed to meeting our corporate social responsibilities (CSR), we at SPRG not only assist clients in developing their own CSR campaigns, but also directly offer CSR and pro bono services to institutions, associations and charitable organisations. What’s more, in 2014, we launched Strategic CSR Network Limited (SCSR), which became the only NGO in Hong Kong to be fully funded and supported by a PR agency. SCSR operates as a platform for serving the underprivileged in Hong Kong on an ongoing basis. Specifically, it leverages the existing businesses and social networks of SPRG; connecting and bringing together different parties, including corporations, NGOs, charities and volunteers to achieve common goals.
