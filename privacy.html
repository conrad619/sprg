Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to your name (“Personal Information”). We do not sell, lease, or disclose your Personal Information to third parties.

Similar to many other websites, we also collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Website (“Log Data”).

This Log Data may include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, location, browser type, browser, operating system, screen resolution, pages you visit, total number of your visits, frequency of visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on our Website, where you visit from, and other statistics. This helps us to under our Website users better to build a better user experience. Some of this data will be aggregated or statistical, and this means we will not be able to identify you individually.

In addition, we may use third party services such as Google Analytics that collect, monitor and analyse the data to further improve the user experience and contents of our Website.

Communications

We may use your Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that might be of interest to you.

COOKIES

Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Like many sites, we use “cookies” to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Website.

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, however take note that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

NOTICE TO USERS UNDER 18 YEARS OLD

Our Website is intended for use by users 18 years old and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the 18 years old through the Website, thus if we learn about such personal information, we will delete the information from our database. Please do not send us any personal information if you are below 18 years old. If you would like to contact us, please do so through a parent or legal guardian. If you are a parent or legal guardian of a child under 18 years old who has submitted his/her personal information to our Website, please email us at enquiry@sprg.com.hk, and we will delete the data from our database as soon as possible.
